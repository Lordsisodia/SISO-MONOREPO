## Strategic Overview

**Primary Business Goal:**
The Academy section should transform new partners into revenue-generating SISO affiliates through structured education, practical tools, and clear progression pathways that accelerate their path to the $50K earnings target.

**Multiple Business Goals:**
- Transform new partners into revenue-generating affiliates
- Provide structured education and practical tools  
- Create clear progression pathways to $50K target
- Reduce support tickets through self-service learning
- Increase partner retention and engagement
- Build scalable onboarding system

## Critical User Journeys

**Top 3 Most Important Paths:**
1. **Onboarding Journey**: New partner signup → Getting Started → First courses → Initial earnings
2. **Skill Building Journey**: Course consumption → Portfolio building → Client acquisition
3. **Advanced Growth Journey**: Specialized training → Recruitment → Leadership roles

- 60% enroll in 2+ courses
